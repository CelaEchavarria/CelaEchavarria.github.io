About Me

Hello World!

I went to Stevens Institute of Technology and obtained a Computer Engineering B.E and a Networked Information Systems M.E. After a few years working in technical roles and business related roles, I felt the need to switch careers. After much soul searching, informational interviews and taking a few online classes in statistics and python, I realized that the field of Data Science would be the best fit as it combines technical skills and my interest in business.

I was fortunate to be chosen as one of the Transcend Scholarship recipients at Udacity and got my Data Analyst Nanodegree on September 6, 2016. I am now ready to tackle my next career move.

I love to travel and photography. I am passionate about helping underrepresented youth pursue higher level education and explore the countless opportunities available to them.

I am open to job opportunities and volunteer opportunities that align with my passions.
